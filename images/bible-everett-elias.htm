Family Bible of Elias Everett (1817-1885)

These pages are scans the original family Bible of Elias Everett made in 2007.  As far as can be determined, this is the history of the Bible.  George Nathan Everett received the Bible from his father in 1882 (see note inside front cover).  Alice Everett Bassett received the Bible after he died in 1894.  Her family kept the Bible after her death in 1917 and Edythe Bassett Conaway had the Bible in the 1970's when I (Steve Baskauf) first saw it.  After Edythe's death in 1982 it was given James H. Bassett who then gave it to Steve (Bassett) Baskauf in the late 1980's.  It is currently in his possession.  Contact Steve Baskauf
