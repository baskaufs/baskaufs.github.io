barn 
In this photo and the house photo to the right, Amy Mason Everett is on the left and Margaret Mason (wife of Donald) is on the right.  Both photos were taken on 1950 Aug 21. Written on photo margin: "(little stream here where my grandmother got rattle snake bite) same barn".  (This was in the writing of Amy Mason Everett who refers to Clarinda Edgecomb Snyder, her grandmother and the mother of Amy Snyder Mason.)  Also written "Breese Farm, Shawnee Twp."
house 
Written on margin in same handwriting: "at milk house as it was when she lived here".  On the back was written "Old milk house at Mom's home farm"
Mason-farm 
M.D. (Marquis D.) and Amy Louisa (Snyder) Mason's home on Bluelick Rd., Lima, OH.  Enlargement of the barn shows "MD Mason 1905" painted on it.  "House was white with dark green trim.  Barn was slate color with white trim.  Amy Mason Everett described it this way when she lived there." (comments by Shirly Everett-Clark)
