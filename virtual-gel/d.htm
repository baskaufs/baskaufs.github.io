Parents of Lansil Edgecomb

Lansil Edgecomb (c. 1815) and Malinda M. Russell

Key words: Lansil Edgecomb, Malinda Russell Edgecomb, Malinda Edgecomb, Malinda M. Edgecomb, Lansil and Malinda Edgecomb, Lansil and Malinda Russell Edgecomb, Lauson Edgecomb

Lansil married Malinda M. Russell on 2 (or 6) Feb 1837 in Trumbull Co., OH.  Lansil purchased land next to his father Uriah in Bath Township, Allen Co., OH in 1833. In 1835 he bought land in Monroe Twp. which at that time was in Putnam Co., but adjacent to Bath Twp. He moved to Wisconsin sometime between 1854 and 1860 and was a carpenter. Lansil died 1870.

Lansil had the following children 1 2 3:

Maryetta (Jacobs), b. 6 Nov 1837 in Ohio, m. 20 Dec 1855 to William L. Jacobs
?Marcellia (Delany) Lived in Evansville, IN in 1894.
?Ann (Paugh) Lived in Delphos, OH in 1894.
Ezra b. 1845 in Allen Co., OH, m. 22 Jun 1867 Margaret J. Bartholomew Edgecomb
Rufus 22 Feb 1848 in Allen Co., OH - 30 May 1918, m. (1) 24 Oct 1869 Permelia F. Bartholomew, m. (2) Mary E. Cliff Edgecomb.
Louisa Elizabeth (Ricks) b. 4 Jan 1850 in Allen Co., OH, m. 24 Oct 1869 William H. Ricks
Sarah Ann (Clark) b. 1854/5 in OH, m. 30 Sep 1877 to William E. Clark
