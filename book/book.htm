The first part of this history describes the relationships among three early pioneer families of Bath Twp., Allen Co., OH, USA. Their origins in Trumbull Co., OH, New York, Connecticut, and Rhode Island are discussed. Many descendants of the families are outlined in the second part of the book. However, to protect the privacy of individuals listed in the book, this online version contains only the parts of the book that do not deal with living descendants.   The book is available online at (Lulu.com).
